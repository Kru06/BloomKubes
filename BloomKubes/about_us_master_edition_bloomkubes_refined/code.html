Founded in 2014 by Eleanor Sage and Julian Cream, the Living Archive began as a simple experiment in a reclaimed Victorian glasshouse. Today, it stands as a testament to their belief that luxury and ecology are not mutually exclusive.

“We didn’t want to just grow plants; we wanted to curate an experience that honors the patience of nature while embracing the efficiency of the modern world.”

— Eleanor Sage
